Boiler Service
What is included

A service of your Boiler completed by a Gas Safe registered engineer, including: An inspection of the Boiler and its installation completed in accordance with the current Gas Safety (Installation C Use) Regulations and the manufacturer’s instructions. This includes, where required;
• Testing, checking and cleaning of the Boiler and its system filter.
• Maintenance parts fitted at either no additional cost or additional cost depending on the plan purchased.
• Testing the gases produced by the Boiler, its ventilation and the effectiveness of the flue
• Determining whether the Boiler is safe to use

Some system filters are superior in their quality to others. We will advise on an individual basis if the system filter has an increased risk of component failure. Should we identify a lower quality filter on your system, we will ask if you would like us to clean the filter after explaining the risks.
Should the filter or filter seals fail, repair/replacement would be an additional cost and outside of this agreement.
In this event, we would supply you with a quote for the repair/replacement, should you wish to proceed, your authorisation before undertaking any further works will be required.
If the filter isolation valves fail to isolate, we will be unable to clean the filter and a repair to the isolation valves will be required. This would be outside of this agreement and the same process for quotations as above would apply.
We will replace the large main seal, should they fail on the following filters:

• Adey Magnaclean mk1
• Adey Magnaclean mk2

Exclusions

Any Breakdown or Repair needed to the Boiler or your Heating System (found during a service)
All further works required to undertake planned maintenance are subject to additional cost E.G. faulty filter isolation valves or faulty pumps etc.
Maintenance that is not part of the standard manufacturer’s service unless specified in your plan (plan details can be found in the service plans document provided in the M.B.S Members pack or on our website www.mrboilerservices.co.uk)
A test of the Gas Supply Pipe to the Boiler, unless there is a known or suspected escape of gas or the boiler service disturbs certain joints in which a test of the supply pipe is required under the gas regulations.
The following are also excluded from the plans and therefore Mr Boiler Services will not be liable for any of the following:

  • systems/equipment/appliances that have not been installed, serviced or maintained regularly according to British Standards and/or manufacturer’s instructions; or that are subject to a manufacturer’s recall
  • Any defect, damage or breakdown caused by malicious or deliberate action, negligence, misuse or third party interference, including any attempted repair or modification to the elements covered by this plan, which does not comply with British Standards
  • 3. Any situation where due to health and safety, a specialist person is required, e.g. where asbestos is present.

Cancelling a booked boiler service

If you choose to cancel your boiler service booking (at any time), please be aware the later you look to reschedule the booking, the less likely you are to obtain your preferred
alternative date as the diary is live and everchanging. Bookings need to be cancelled no less than 2 hours before the appointment. Our 3-strike policy is applied here.
On rare occasions we may need to reschedule your booking. We will always contact via phone before following up with a confirmation email of the cancellation. This contact could be before the appointment time or on the time of appointment. We will then make an alternative booking with you over the phone. In this instance, we will strive to make the rescheduled appointment as soon as possible, either on another service day (Monday’s or Tuesday’s) or an alternative day.

About your Boiler Service

All services will take place after 12 months of paid cover. Boiler Services can be completed any time throughout the year, excluding during our booked annual leave which will be shown in the diary when booking, but we only allow boiler service bookings on Monday’s and Tuesday’s unless, an agreed date with us has been arranged in which we can book on an alternative day. The best way to organise an alternative day booking would be for you to call us.
We will send service reminders for the boiler service via an email in the 4 weeks prior to the due date. If you do not book in, either before or after the reminders are sent to you, and your Boiler service isn’t completed during the 12-month period, you won’t be eligible for a refund. We can carry out the service at any point after the due date but refunds are not given. If this rolls into the next 12 month period, the service can be booked and completed but a refund for the year in which the service was missed will not be refundable. Normal payments can continue and the original service date when the plan was purchased will be adhered to meaning the interval between the 1st and 2nd service could be less than 12 months in this instance.
Services are required every 12 months, however during periods of high demand you may find the dates and times in which you are available have already been booked. If this occurs, please contact us as we will assist in arranging a booking as close as possible, which could be on days which are not set out in our diary for servicing. There is no guarantee we will be able to accommodate your service within the timeframes required as our diary is a live and everchanging.
Where safety defects are identified, the current Gas Industry Unsafe Situation Procedure will be followed. This may mean that the Boiler cannot be used until the problem we find is resolved. The cost of repair including parts and labour is not covered in this plan, Unless this faulty part is a service part and is covered under your service plan. Details of whats covered is outlined in the “included” listings within the plans.
We can if requested, supply a quotation for any repair. You are under no obligation to utilise our services to carry out the repair although, if required by regulations, a suitable gas safe registered engineer will be needed to carry out the repair.
Please make sure that you have the manufacturer’s instructions available for the engineer when they attend. We will supply you with an A4 document folder labelled M.B.S. Service Plans Members Pack. We have supplied these folders as a good place in which all documents pertaining to the boiler could be kept.

Terms of Service

Duration: The basic Plan requires a minimum commitment of 12 months, continuing a rolling monthly basis thereafter. The Advanced, Boiler Ultimate and Unvented Ultimate plans require a minimum commitment of 24 months continuing a monthly rolling basis thereafter.
We reserve the right to adjust the monthly cost of the plans at our discretion. We will write to you 30 days ahead of the increase. We will not retrospectively charge if you are half way through the year between services. The updated charges will be valid 30 days after notifying you. Our goal is to ensure we keep our customers happy and so rest assured, these adjustments of the monthly price will not be made lightly or regularly.

Changing your address

If you move home and need to update your correspondence address, call us or write to us . You are responsible for informing Mr Boiler Services of a change of address.
A new service plan agreement will need to be made for the boiler at the new address with a service date to be determined. Once determined, payments made under the service plan for the boiler at the previous address will be allocated to the boiler service plan for the boiler at the new address. If there is not 12 months of payments made before the service of the boiler at the new address, then a top up payment equivalent to the bring the balance up to that of 12 monthly payments will be required. Once the service has been carried out on the boiler at the new address, regular monthly payments can then continue as normal with no top up payments required.
If we deem the new property’s boiler not eligible, a refund will be given for the payments made within the 12 month period since the last service, or initial sign on.

Missing payment

If you fail to make a payment for your Plan on the due date, your Plan will be suspended and you will not be entitled to any service(s) under this Plan until payment is made. If you do not pay the requested amount within 30 days of the due date, your Plan will be cancelled. You will not be liable for the remaining cost of the 12 month period but refunds for any funds already paid will not be given.

Creating access

You will be required to make reasonable access to the boiler and all other points of interest whilst carrying out your service E.G radiators, cylinders and airing cupboard etc. Our engineers will be vigilant with health and safety matters, if they deem the area unsafe due to access or any other health and safety concern, the service will be cancelled and rescheduled. With all matters such as these we run a 3-strike policy. If we reschedule twice, and upon the third visit we are still unable to carry out the service, we will cancel the annual service, the service plan and no refunds will be given.

Cancellations

How can you cancel

If you wish to cancel your Plan; you or an authorised representative, you must contact us in writing either by post or email. No refunds will be made so the best way to cancel the policy is to advise us that after the next service, you will be cancelling the service plan and future payments. This way you will have paid for the current boiler service and will not lose any further payments.

How can Mr Boiler Services cancel

Mr Boiler Services reserves the right to cancel your Boiler Service Plan prior to the service being carried out, by giving you at least 7 days' notice at your last known address. In these circumstances Mr Boiler Services will refund the amount paid since the last service or initial purchase.
